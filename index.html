Luc Bertin


Paris, FR.

French freelance and graduate engineer, passionate about Data Science and AI, who loves coding in Python.

Since completion of my studies on September 2019, I create contents to teach MSc students Python programming, ML and DL concepts, so to both improve my theoretical knowledge and be able to better communicate about it. I also provide courses on Docker and the concept of Linux namespaces, but got increasingly involved in this tech for CI pipelines and deployments. When I’m not teaching, I’m on clients’ missions.
